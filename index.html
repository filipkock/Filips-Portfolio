WHO IS THIS DUDE?

Hello there! My name is Filip Lee Kockendal and I'm a third-year student with a keen interest in applied IT and interaction design. My passion for exploring how humans interact with technology motivated me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Cognitive Science at the University of Gothenburg. There I've gained a deep understanding of how people, machines, and technology process and use information. As a lifelong learner and problem solver, I'm constantly seeking new challenges to apply my skills and continue learning.

MY EXPERIENCE

My design experience is varied, having explored different tools and techniques. In addition to my design skills, I also possess experience in the programming languages HTML, CSS and Python as well as data visualisation tools such as Power BI.
